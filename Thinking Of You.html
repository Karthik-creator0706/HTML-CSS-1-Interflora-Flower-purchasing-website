    Thinking of You Flowers & Gifts FAQs

    Thinking of you flowers delivery: what are popular choices?

    When it comes to sending flowers to a friend, you want to be sure to pick something bright and full of life, and something that will really spice up their space. A great floral option for moments like these is a beautiful bouquet of stunning sunflowers. Sunflowers are reminiscent of the sun, so maybe they remind you of the warmth your friend brings to your life.

    Nothing says “you are in my thoughts” like a surprise rose delivery just because. Maybe you sense that a friend is feeling a little blue, or a co-worker could use a pick-me-up. It is situations just like these where Proflowers shines. You can count on us for the biggest selection of fresh-cut blooms that are sure to brighten anyone’s day. Choose from elegant lilies, sweet carnations, daisies, tulips, and so much more!

    What are nice thinking of you bouquets to send to a partner?

    Sending thinking of you flowers to your partner needs to have a little bit more thought than if you were just sending them to a friend. When it comes to your partner, you want to be sure that you are choosing more romantic flowers, like long stem roses or orchids. When it comes down to it, you know your partner best, so be sure to pick whichever floral arrangements reminds you of them the most.

    Can I send a thinking of you flower arrangement anywhere in the country?

    The simple answer is yes! You can send a hand-arranged bouquet of flowers anywhere in the country because Proflowers has a wide network of local florists throughout the states. Rest easy knowing that we can deliver your floral arrangement to just about any address. All you have to do is enter your ZIP code at the top of this page to ensure that we do indeed deliver near that special someone’s area of the world.

    What gifts can I send along with a thinking of you bouquet?

    One of the best gifts you can send along with a thinking of you bouquet is a heartfelt note, where you would be using your words to express your feelings and thoughts about the recipient. Another great gift to include with your flowers and notes is a gift basket of chocolates. You just can’t go wrong with yummy treats, kind words, and beautiful flowers.

    What message should I include with my thinking of you gift?

    It can be difficult to express your feelings on paper when sending a bouquet of flowers for no special reason. Maybe you want the recipient to understand that no matter the physical distance between you, they are always in your thoughts.

    Let them know you truly care by including a thoughtful, personalized message. Here are some ideas to help get you started:

  • I love to see beautiful things come to beautiful people – and this is one of those times.
  • I know life has thrown you some curveballs lately. I just wanted you to know you are in my thoughts and prayers.
  • I hope these flowers put a smile on your face because I know you could use a pick me up!
  • It's been too long since our paths have crossed. I wanted you to know that I am thinking only good thoughts about you!
  • I cannot imagine life without you by my side. Wishing you all the best!
  • Every time you look at these blooms, remember that I am thinking of you.
  • I hope these beautiful flowers make your day extraordinary.
  • You are and always will be the love of my life.
  • I don’t need a special reason to say I love you.
  • Just wanted you to know you are appreciated.
