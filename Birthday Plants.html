    Birthday Plants FAQs

    Birthdays only come once a year, which is why you need to make sure their special day is one to remember. The best way to help your loved one celebrate another trip around the sun is with birthday plants that will light up the room. Whether you choose a lucky bamboo plant, gardenias, roses, or exotic orchids, our selection of happy birthday plants will bring a smile to any guest of honor.

    What are the best plants to get for a birthday?

    The best plants to get for a birthday are ones that radiate joy and happiness. What better way to brighten up one’s big day than with a vibrant sunflower bouquet? Or, choose an elegant hanging spider plant that is easy to take care of and will add a splash of green to any room. At Proflowers, our birthday plant delivery is second to none. We have plenty of happy birthday flowers and birthday gift baskets that are suitable for any birthday guy or gal in your life. We even have a variety of same-day delivery options to choose from, so you don’t show up to the party empty-handed!

    What gifts pair well with birthday plants?

    If you’re getting birthday plants delivered straight to the doorstep of your best friend or family member, you might as well tack on a second gift that pairs well with it! Flowers are a great way to show your love and appreciation for those closest to you in life. But if you want to make their birthday even more special, send along one of our birthday gift baskets filled with delectable chocolates, juicy fruits, and salty nuts to give their taste buds a party, too. Aside from flowering plants, you can send your birthday wishes with our mouth-watering cakes, classic chocolate chip cookies, and one-of-a-kind cake pops! With gifts like these, the party never has to end.

    Can plants be delivered for a birthday?

    Yes! Birthday plants are a perfect way to show you care for loved ones both near and far. If you need to arrange a birthday plant delivery, we take special care when sending them, packaging them securely so they won’t be damaged. Our team of experts ensures your plants will be delivered on time and in top condition. We can organize the delivery of flowering plants, plants in baskets, potted plants, and much more. They are fantastic birthday gifts that last a long time. Your loved one can look at the plant and think of you for years to come.

    What does a plant symbolize as a birthday gift?

    Finding the perfect gift doesn’t have to be difficult when there are so many options. Birthday gifts should be meaningful, and plants are a great choice because they’re good for your health and add beauty to the home. Unlike some other gift choices, they’re also sustainable and live a long and happy life if looked after properly.

    Giving a plant to someone can signify respect, and like flowers, they can also symbolize different meanings. Here’s a few to consider when you’re looking for the perfect gift:

  • Lucky bamboo plant: Symbolizes prosperity, luck, love, and longevity.
  • Peace lily: Symbolizes innocence, tranquility, rebirth, and purity. Sending this means you’re offering your support and love.
  • Mother of Pearl Rose: Represents family love, making it an excellent choice for a brother or sister or even favorite cousin.
  • Tulips: Symbolize perfect love, with different colors representing different meanings—an inspired choice for your wife or husband on their birthday.
  • Succulent plants: Symbolizes selflessness and love, expressing your unconditional love to someone special in your life.

    • You can also find the plant or flower for their birth month for an extra thoughtful touch. For example, daisies are for April, while birthday roses are for June. If you’re buying for someone whose birthday is in January, a carnation could be a perfect choice.

    Why is a birthday plant the perfect gift?

    The last thing you want is a shot-in-the-dark gift that the recipient will forget about or return. Instead, give the gift of life with a bouquet of flowers that will add charm to any table, desk, or counter. A birthday plant is a perfect gift because they’re unique, thoughtful, and spread beauty wherever they’re placed. Whether it’s for an aunt with a green thumb or a friend who wants to add decor to their new apartment, a birthday plant will go a long way. Shop all plants today to find the perfect gift for your loved one.
