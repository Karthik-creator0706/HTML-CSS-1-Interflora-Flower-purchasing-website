    Gift Baskets FAQs

    What are good gift basket ideas?

    Everyone loves a good gift basket, but the best ones are those that are relevant to the person you're giving it to. In other words, a gift basket should be somehow connected to the person you're giving it to and the event or occasion that calls for the gift basket in the first place. It should be filled with things that the recipient will love.

    For instance, if your Mom enjoys a little luxury, you might consider getting her a spa gift basket on her birthday or Mother's Day to celebrate her, show her love, and express appreciation for all that she does. Whatever the occasion may be, just know that Proflowers has gift baskets for all special events and holidays.

    What to put in a gift basket?

    Ideally, a gift basket should be made up of items that the recipient will enjoy. Accordingly, our specially curated gift baskets for delivery are guaranteed to please. If someone you know has a sweet tooth, send a gift basket filled with gourmet chocolate. Or, if they lean more on the savory side, send a gift basket stuffed with melt-in-your-mouth meats and cheeses. Whatever your friend or loved one enjoys, we can help you build a gift basket that they'll never forget.

    How to make a gift basket?

    To make a gift basket, you need to collect a number of little items that mesh well together and complement one another. After all, you probably want to send a sleek gift basket, not just a mish-mash of random items. Therefore, it's often helpful to pick a theme before putting together a gift basket. For instance, if you want to make a gourmet sweets gift basket, you might combine some candy bouquets, candy gift baskets, truffles, and other rich chocolates into an elegant arrangement. You can also shop our collection, which features a number of gourmet chocolate gift baskets for delivery.

    What about savory options?

    In addition to our wide range of sweet treat baskets, we also offer plenty of savory options. Our meat and cheese baskets are perfect for a picnic on a sunny day to celebrate a special occasion. Our Gourmet Fruit, Cheese and Nut Crate is an excellent choice for spoiling someone special in your life. It comes with an array of delicious orchard fruits, cheeses, crackers, and roasted salted pistachios in a special gift crate.

    Do you have gift baskets without food?

    Yes! In addition to our vast range of food gift baskets, we also offer a wide range of spa gift baskets if you're looking to pamper someone. Why not consider our Lavender Spa Day Gift Basket? It comes with comfy slippers and lots of lavender goodies, including hand soap, a candle, and a relaxing bath bomb. If your special someone loves the smell of lavender this is the perfect gift!

    Our Palisades Canyon's curated Refreshing Retreat Care Package is another excellent option to make your loved one feel extra special. The package includes comfy fuzzy socks, soothing bath salts, and delicious butter cookies. It is the perfect combination for someone who loves to relax at home.

    Do you have healthy food options?

    While many of our gift delivery baskets are full of sweets and snacks, we also have plenty of healthy options, too. At Proflowers, our fruit gift baskets offer a range of delicious and seasonal fruit that can be delivered anywhere you like. If you're looking for a combination of healthy foods with a little bit of a treat, why not consider our Fresh Favorites gift box. This gift basket comes with an assortment of ripe fruit and a mix of candies and chocolate-covered goodies.

    When should I send a gift basket?

    Honestly, you can send a gift basket anytime! While we have themed gift baskets for special occasions — such as birthdays, baby showers, and bereavements - you can send a basket for other reasons, too. Send a gift basket as a housewarming gift or pair a basket with some just because flowers as a random act of kindness for a friend or loved one. Organize your gift basket delivery today.
