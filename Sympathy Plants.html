    Sympathy Plants FAQs

    Is sending sympathy plants to someone a nice way of showing support?

    When someone you love is going through a hardship, it can be challenging to find the right words to say to show your support. But actions speak louder than words, and sending sympathy plants is a great way to express your condolences. They will brighten up a room and hopefully brighten up your loved one’s spirits as well. Send one to show you are there for them.

    What are common sympathy plants to send to someone?

    These arrangements usually consist of dark green plants and white flowers. The dark green color symbolizes respect while the white flowers convey purity, innocence, and re-birth. You can also send Thinking of You flowers, which consist of more colorful arrangements. Either are great sympathy gifts that let a loved one know you’re thinking of them.

    Why are roses popular funeral gifts?

    Roses are a common flower to send during the loss of a loved one. You can often find funeral roses in large bouquets, wreaths, or funeral sprays. They have a universal meaning of love and can show both grief and joy as you celebrate the life of the deceased while still mourning their loss. Different colors can also represent different feelings. For example, white roses represent innocence and safe passing, while red roses represent deep love and grief. Finally, yellow roses are symbolic of friendship.

    How do I know my sympathy plant delivery will arrive safely?

    We always take the proper measures to ensure your sympathy plant delivery will arrive safely and right to your loved one’s doorstep. All of them come with a 7-day guarantee, but if you’re looking for something to last longer, we also have house plants and funeral wreaths that you can send to show your support. We utilize our partnerships with thousands of local florists around the nation, so you can rest assured that your plant won’t be traveling far. It helps reduce the amount of stress on your plant, keeping it happy and healthier for longer.

    What else shows condolences along with sympathy plants?

    We offer a variety of sympathy gifts. If you want to do more, you can add baked goods and other snacks to the delivery to make sure they’re well-fed during their difficult time. With every delivery, you can include a personal message that further shows your condolences. You can also shop our extensive sympathy collection to find an array of memorial plants, floral arrangements, condolence baskets, and more.

    Can I have sympathy plants delivered on the same day?

    Most losses are unexpected, so planning ahead isn’t an option. But that doesn’t mean you have to wait to show your friend, family, or spouse that you care. Proflowers offers numerous plants for sympathy gifts that are available for same day delivery. This is great if you live out of town but want your loved one to know you are there in spirit or if you’ve been caught up in the emotion and grief and haven’t had time to order.

    Can Proflowers send sympathy plants to funeral homes?

    Yes. At Proflowers, we know that times of loss can be challenging, so we can take care of the flowers for you. Order your funeral sprays and funeral rose delivery, and our partner florists will help coordinate delivery directly to the funeral home.
